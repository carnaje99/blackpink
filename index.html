Black Pink had a gorgeous debut report on August 8, 2016 with the single 'SQUARE ONE'. As a girl group that was introduced after 7 years at YG Entertainment, it announced the birth of a large group of rookies by ranking first on various music charts.

Since then, Black Pink has grown into a global girl group representing K-Pop, releasing its first mini-album 'SQUARE UP'. This spring, Black Pink is taking another leap forward with an EP album of the same name as the title song 'Kill This Love'.

In January 2019, the music video for "Ddu-Du Ddu-Du" became the most-watched music video by a South Korean group, surpassing BTS' "DNA". In November 2019, the video surpassed one billion views. In September 2019, Blackpink became the music group with the highest number of subscribers on YouTube after surpassing British boy band One Direction.

Blackpink made it into Time Magazine's "Time 100 Next", which is a list of 100 rising stars, launched this year by Time Magazine.

On November 22, 2019, Blackpink reached 9,604,061 followers on Spotify, surpassing Fifth Harmony and Little Mix, breaking the record as the most followed girl group.

Forbes Korea Power Celebrity has named Blackpink the most influential celebrities of Korea in 2019 and the third most influential celebrities in Korea in 2020.
